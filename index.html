About

Cynthia Roberts

Cynthia Roberts is the talented creator of Country Collections, located in the heart of the Niagara Region, Ontario Canada.

Cynthia creates an assortment of fun, unique, one of kind designs in the way of jean purses, window art and garden art.

She has a creative eye with oodles of design ideas. Besides creating fun ‘stuff’, Cynthia loves gardening, golfing, power walking, photography and reading.

And then of course there are her two awesome grandchildren!

Collections

Country Collections Offers the Following

JEAN PURSES: are made from re-cycled jeans with a bit of a bling factor for added interest and a unique one of a kind look. The purses are available in a variety of sizes suitable for the young princess, as well as teens and ladies.

Pocket purses slung over the shoulder are just the right size for the iPhone and a couple of credit cards. Water bottle slings are perfect for hiking, the long power walk or a workout at the gym.

Check out the “Walkin’ the Dog” bag. It carries a water bottle as well as storage for easy access to the poop bags! Tote bags and “Bummer” bags (duffle) are also available.

Items are individually priced starting at $15.00. Shipping extra.

WINDOW ART: is created from old window frames to make indoor and outdoor one-off designs. The windows come in a variety of sizes and each creation is uniquely designed.

Prices are individually priced. Shipping extra.

GARDEN ART: is created from antiques, old stuff and other items of interest to make distinctive pieces of ‘art’ to hang or place in the garden.

Custom orders are welcome.
